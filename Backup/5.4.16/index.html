What you need to knows before signing up for a Credit Card
Written by Ruchi Gupta
Image courtesy of Unsplashed
Credits cards have always elicited divided opinions from financial experts and thought leaders. While others insist it is one of the biggest innovations in the financial sector, others cannot stomach the financial burdens it can bring about, if misused. Below is everything you need to know before taking advantage of its benefits and being exposed to its common traps.
What is a Credit Card ?
Credits cards have always elicited divided opinions from financial experts and thought leaders. While others insist it is one of the biggest innovations in the financial sector, others cannot stomach the financial burdens it can bring about, if misused. Below is everything you need to know before taking advantage of its benefits and being exposed to its common traps.
Who can use Credit Cards
Credit cards are for anyone who is above 18 years with a regular source of income. However, one must still prove beyond reproach that they are capable of paying back any amount borrowed before being approved by credit card companies. Credit card history which details one’s credit borrowing history and payment is one of the things that companies pay close attention to, before approving credit card requests. While chances of being approved for a credit card are usually high as long as one has a stable source of income, they are still not everyone. Credit cards are not for people who lack self-control when it comes to finances and spending. They are also not for people trying to repair their finances as they are likely to lead to more debts.
